ABOUT GUERNSEY ARCHITECTURAL SOLUTIONS, INC

Established in 2007 in DeLand, Florida, Guernsey Architectural Solutions, Inc is a single source partner. We perform our own ACM panel fabrication and installation to allow for better coordination and full accountability. This process results in a quality product delivered on time, normally at a lower cost.

We also specialize in design implementation and installation of other metal systems, such as corrugated screens, decorative laser-cut panels, louver systems, and more!

We are HGI certified as well as a certified distributor, fabricator and installer with all major North American Manufacturers: Mitsubishi Plastics Composites America, Inc (Alpolic*), Alcoa Architectural Products (Reynobond*), Alucoil North America (Larson*), Fairfield Metal LLC (Vitrabond*), Dri-Design*, Berridge Manufacturing Co., Kingspan Group Company (Morin*), Longboard* Architectural Products.

Guernsey Architectural Solutions brings 35+ years of installation experience, 30+ years of project management experience, and 20+ years of manufacturing experience, which will ensure a quality product for your project. Material fabrication in our state-of-the-art shop is easily completed with our computer-controlled CNC routers, and CNC mills.
